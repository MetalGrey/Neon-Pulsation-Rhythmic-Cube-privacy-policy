Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding this data when using our game.

2. Data Collection

We may collect the following data when you use our game:

  • Device IP address: to determine geographical location and monitor user activity.
  • Device model: to analyze game performance on different devices.
  • Error logs: to identify and fix issues encountered by users.

3. Data Usage

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Monitoring and fixing errors: we analyze error logs to improve the stability and performance of the game.
  • Performance analysis: device model data helps us optimize the game for various hardware configurations.
  • Security: IP addresses are used to ensure security and prevent fraud.

4. Data Storage

The collected data is stored on our secure servers and is accessible only to authorized personnel. We take reasonable measures to protect the data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Disclosure

We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties, except when required by law or necessary to protect our rights.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete data (in certain cases).
  • Restrict the processing of your data.
  • Transfer data to another organization.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact details provided below.

7. Cookies

We do not use cookies to collect data.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page. Please check this page regularly to stay informed of any changes.

9. Contact Information
